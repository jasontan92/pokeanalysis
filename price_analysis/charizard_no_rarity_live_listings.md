## Important Notes

### About "Kairiki" (かいりき) Charizard
The "Kairiki Charizard" refers to an ERROR variant where the card incorrectly lists the Pokemon's profile as "Kairiki Pokemon, Height 1.5m, Weight 70.5kg" instead of the correct "Flame Pokemon, Height 1.7m, Weight 90.5kg". This error occurred because Machoke's (Goriky/Kairiki) profile was accidentally printed on early No Rarity Charizard cards. This misprint combined with the No Rarity Symbol makes these cards extremely valuable.

### Authentication
- Always verify authenticity before purchasing high-value cards
- PSA, BGS, and CGC are trusted grading services
- Raw cards carry more risk - examine closely or request additional photos
