## How to Identify No Rarity Symbol

1. **Card Number:** Should be **#068** (NOT #003)
2. **No Star Symbol:** Bottom right corner has NO rarity mark
3. **Copyright Date:** 1996 on card
4. **Set:** 拡張パック (Expansion Pack) - First Japanese set

## Buying Tips

1. **Verify No Rarity:** Always confirm #068 and absence of ★ symbol
2. **Check PSA Cert Number:** Verify on [PSA Cert Verification](https://www.psacard.com/certverification)
3. **Use Proxy Services:** For Yahoo Japan/Mercari Japan
   - [Buyee](https://buyee.jp) - Most popular
   - [Zenmarket](https://zenmarket.jp)
   - [Sendico](https://www.sendico.com)
4. **Factor in Costs:**
   - International shipping: $15-50
   - Proxy service fees: 5-10%
   - Import duties/taxes: Varies by country
