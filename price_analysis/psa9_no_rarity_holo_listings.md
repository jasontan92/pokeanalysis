## TIPS FOR HUNTING

1. **Set up eBay alerts** for "no rarity symbol PSA 9" and individual Pokemon names
2. **Check Fanatics Collect daily** - new inventory appears regularly
3. **Monitor PSA auction prices** for recent sale comps
4. **Current price ranges**:
   - PSA 9: $2,250 (Hitmonchan) to $14,999 (Blastoise)
   - PSA 8: $677 (Magneton) to $49,995 (Charizard)
   - CGC 8.5-10: $799 (Ninetales CGC 8.5) to $35,000 (Venusaur CGC 10)
5. **Verify PSA cert numbers** at https://www.psacard.com/cert
6. **Search with quotes**: Use "no rarity symbol" in quotes to filter out regular base set cards
7. **Check lowpopping.com** - specialty site for low population graded cards (listings sell fast)
8. **Value plays**: PSA 8s often 50-70% cheaper than PSA 9s for same card
