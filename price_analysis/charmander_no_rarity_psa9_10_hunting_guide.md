## TIPS FOR HUNTING

1. **Set up alerts** on eBay, Mercari, and Yahoo Auctions for these specific cards
2. **Check Card Rush daily** - they frequently update inventory
3. **Monitor magi** - Japanese collectors often list rare items here
4. **Use FromJapan or Buyee** as proxy services for Yahoo Auctions
5. **PSA 10 cards are extremely rare** - be prepared to pay premium prices
6. **Verify authenticity** - fake PSA cases exist, buy from reputable sellers
