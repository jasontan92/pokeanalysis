## Notes

- **No Rarity cards** have NO symbol in the bottom right corner
- Look for `第1弾拡張パック` (First Expansion Pack) - these are the original 1996 prints
- `初版` means "first edition" which correlates with no rarity
- `★` in Japanese listings indicates RARE rarity (not no rarity)
- `◆` indicates UNCOMMON
- No symbol = Common OR No Rarity (depending on card)
