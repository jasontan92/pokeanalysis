## CARD INFORMATION

### Raichu No Rarity - Base Set (1996)
- **Japanese Name:** ライチュウ
- **Card Number:** No.026 (printed as No.025 on some first editions - ERROR)
- **Level:** LV.40
- **HP:** 80
- **Rarity:** No rarity mark (初版/マークなし)
- **Set:** Pocket Monsters Card Game Expansion Pack (第1弾拡張パック)
- **Release:** October 20, 1996
- **Type:** Holo (キラ)

### Identifying First Edition No Rarity
- No star (★), circle (●), or diamond (◆) rarity symbol in bottom right corner
- No expansion symbol
- Old back design (旧裏面)
- Some prints have incorrect Pokedex number (025 instead of 026)
